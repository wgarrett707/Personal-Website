will garrett

computer science student at uiuc

Hi, I'm Will Garrett, a computer science undergraduate at the University of Illinois at Urbana-Champaign!

I'm interested in full stack development, a skill which I have honed through working on various websites and platforms, such as Professor Tissenbaum's educational dashboard for classrooms, a startup on-campus called SeriesFarm, and some other small projects (this website, an NGO homepage, etc.).

As I continue to expand my skillset, artificial intelligence also presents great interest to me. I am currently working with a Northwestern lab that is prompt engineering LLMs to convert document segments of network protocol in natural language into finite state diagrams, and I'm implementing an emotion sentiment LLM into our speech analysis system with Professor Tissenbaum.

Most of my experience lies with Python (along with related frameworks such as Django, Flask, Pandas, and BS4) and Javascript (Node.js, React.js, and Express.js), but I've also worked a lot with C++, Kotlin, and Java. I consider myself to be a fast learner and can quickly adapt to any technology stack that a project may be using.

In my free time, I enjoy playing the guitar, working out, skateboarding, and golfing.
